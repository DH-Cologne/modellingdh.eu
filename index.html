Modelling Between Digital and Humanities: Thinking in Practice

white

Our project focuses on scholarly modelling as a formal and informal reasoning strategy across disciplinary boundaries. It aims to bridge between modelling used in research and teaching. While our starting point is in Digital Humanities, we also intend to reach out and benefit from other disciplines and traditions.
